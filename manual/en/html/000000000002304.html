DTC Troubleshooting: S
DTC indicator S:
 A Short in the A/C Pressure Sensor Circuit
  1. Disconnect the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 2 terminal of the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the climate control unit and the A/C pressure sensor.■
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Reconnect climate control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals of the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.There should be 5 V.
Is the voltage as specification?
YES -
Replace the A/C pressure sensor.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector A (22P).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 20 terminal of climate control unit connector A (22P) and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the climate control unit and the A/C pressure sensor.■
NO -
Substitute a known-good climate control unit, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original climate control unit .■
