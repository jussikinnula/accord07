A/T End Cover Removal
Special Tools Required

Mainshaft holder set
 07PAB-0010001

 or 07PAB-0010000
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the ATF cooler inlet line brackets, ATF filter bracket bolt, line bolt, and remove the ATF cooler line/ATF filter (D) and filter brackets (E).
  1. Remove the ATF cooler outlet line (F).
  1. Remove the dipstick guide pipe (G).
  1. Remove the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A, ATF joint pipes, ATF pipe, and gasket.
  1. Remove the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valves B and C, ATF joint pipes, and gasket.
  1. Remove the transmission range switch cover (H).
  1. Remove the transmission range switch harness clamps (I) from the clamp bracket (J), then remove the transmission range switch (K).
  1. Remove the end cover (L), dowel pins, O-rings, and end cover gasket.
  1. Slip the special tool onto the mainshaft.
  1. Engage the park pawl with the park gear.
  1. Cut the lock tab (A) of the each shaft locknut (B) using a chisel (C). Then remove the locknuts and conical spring washers from each shaft.
    NOTE:
    • Countershaft and secondary shaft locknuts have left-hand threads.
    • Keep all of the chiseled particles out of the transmission.
    • Clean the old mainshaft and countershaft locknuts; they are used to install the press fit idler gear on the mainshaft, and park gear on the countershaft.
  1. Remove the special tool from the mainshaft.
  1. Set a two-jaw (or three-jaw) puller (A) on the countershaft (B) by putting a spacer (C) between the puller and countershaft, then remove the park gear (D).
  1. Install a 6 x 1.0 mm bolts (A) on the mainshaft idler gear (B). Set a puller (C) on the mainshaft (D) with putting a spacer (E) between the puller and mainshaft, then remove the mainshaft idler gear.
  1. Remove the park pawl, park pawl spring, park pawl shaft, and stop shaft.
  1. Remove the park lever from the control shaft.
