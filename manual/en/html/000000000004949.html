DTC Troubleshooting: 8
LKAS Camera DTC 8:
 Low Tempature Detection with LKAS Camera
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Set the temperature control dial to Max Hot, the mode control switch to Heat and the recirculation control switch to Recirculate.
  1. Turn the fun switch to Max, and heating for 15 minutes or more.
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Stop the engine.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 8 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose terminals in the LKAS camera 10P connector. If there is not a malfunction, replace the LKAS camera. ■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
