A/T Clutch Pressure Control Solenoid Valve A Replacement
  1. Remove the intake air duct.
  1. Disconnect the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the ATF cooler inlet line brackets (B) from the transmission hanger (C).
  1. Remove the mounting bolts and the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A.
  1. Remove the ATF pipe (D), ATF joint pipes (E), O-rings (F), and gasket (G).
  1. Clean the mounting surface and fluid passages of the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A and transmission housing.
  1. Install the new gasket on the transmission housing, and install the ATF pipe and ATF joint pipes.
  1. Install the new O-rings over the ATF joint pipes.
  1. Install the new A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A.
  1. Secure the ATF cooler inlet line brackets with the bolts on the transmission hanger.
  1. Check the A/T clutch pressure control solenoid valve A connector for rust, dirt, or oil, then connect the connector securely.
  1. Install the intake air duct.
