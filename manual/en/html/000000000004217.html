Distance Switch Test
  1. Remove the switch cover (A).
  1. Remove the distance switch (B), then disconnect the 8P connector (C).
  1. Check for continuity between the 8P connector No. 3 and No. 8 terminals.
    • There should be continuity with the distance switch pressed.
    • There should be no continuity with the distance switch released.
  1. If the continuity check is not as specified, replace the cruise control combination switch assembly.
