Front Fender Trim Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the front fender trim (A).
    -1.
    Using a clip remover, remove the clip (B) securing the front fender trim and cowl cover (C).
    -2.
    Remove the clips (D) from the front grille cover (E).
    -3.
    Pull up the side edge of the front bulkhead cover, then pull up the front fender trim to release the clips (F), and release the hook (G) from the body.
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clip portions into place securely
