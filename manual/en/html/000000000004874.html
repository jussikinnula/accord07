Adaptive Cruise Control/LKAS Combination Switch Replacement
  1. Remove the steering wheel.
  1. Remove the steering wheel rear cover (A).
  1. Remove the voice control switch (B) and the distance/LKAS off switch (C).
  1. Remove the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS combination switch assembly (A).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Do the steering angle sensor neutral position memorization procudure.
