Cruise Control Actuator Test
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector (A) from the cruise control actuator (B).
  1. Remove the cover (A), and check the output linkage (B) for smooth movement.
  1. Connect battery power to the No. 2 terminal and ground to the No. 1 terminal.
  1. Check for a clicking sound from the magnetic clutch. The output linkage should be locked.
  1. If the output linkage is not locked, replace the cruise control actuator assembly.
  1. Check the operation of the actuator motor in each output linkage position according to the table. You should be able to hear the motor.
