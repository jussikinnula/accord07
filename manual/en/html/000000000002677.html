Cruise Control Actuator/Cable Replacement
  1. Loosen the locknut (A), then remove the cruise control cable (B) from the throttle linkage.
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector (A), and remove the actuator cover (B), then remove the bolts securing the cruise control actuator.
  1. Remove the actuator cable from the cruise control actuator.
  1. Install the cruise control actuator and cable in the reverse order of removal, and adjust the free play at the throttle linkage after connecting the cruise control cable.
