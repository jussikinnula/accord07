DTC Troubleshooting: 87-31
DTC 87-31:
 Internal Failure of the OPDS Unit
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on and is DTC 87-31 indicated?
YES -
Replace the OPDS unit. ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Faliure. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC troubleshooting index for troubleshooting details.
