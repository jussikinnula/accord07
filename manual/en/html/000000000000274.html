Door Lock Knob Switch Test - Without Super Locking
Driver's Door Without Super Locking
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 2 and No. 3 (No. 1) terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the LOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the UNLOCK position.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals when the door lock knob switch is in the UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.
    ( ): RHD type
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the door lock actuator.
Front Passenger's Door Without Super Locking
  1. Remove the front passenger's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals when the door lock knob switch in the UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.
  1. If the continuity is not specified, replace the door lock actuator.
Rear Door Without Super Locking
  1. Remove the left or right rear door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals when the door lock knob switch is UNLOCK position and no continuity when the switch is in the LOCK position.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the door lock actuator.
