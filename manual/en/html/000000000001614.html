DTC Troubleshooting: 91
DTC 91:
 VSA Operation
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Disconnect the HDS from the 16P DLC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II) again.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle around a number of corners.
Is DTC 91 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
