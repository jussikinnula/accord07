DTC Troubleshooting: R
DTC indicator R:
 An Open in the A/C Pressure Sensor Circuit
  1. Disconnect the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 9 terminal of climate control unit connector B (16P) and the No. 2 terminal of the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the climate control unit and the A/C pressure sensor.■
  1. Reconnect climate control unit connector B (16P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between the No. 1 and No. 3 terminals of the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.There should be 5 V.
Is the voltage as specification?
YES -
Replace the A/C pressure sensor.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect climate control unit connector A (22P).
  1. Check for continuity between the following terminals of climate control unit connector A (22P) and the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector.
    22P:
    3P:
    No. 15
    No. 3
    No. 20
    No. 1

Is there continuity?
YES -
Check for loose wires or poor connections at climate control unit connector A (22P) and at the A/C pressure sensor 3P connector. If the connections are good, substitute a known-good climate control unit, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original climate control unit .■
NO -
Repair any open in the wire(s) between the climate control unit and the A/C pressure sensor.■
