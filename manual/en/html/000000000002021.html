Fuel Gauge Sending Unit Test
NOTE: For the fuel gauge system circuit diagram, refer to the Gauges Circuit Diagram.
  1. Before starting the fuel gauge sending unit test, perform the multiplex integrated control system troubleshooting test mode A.
    • If no problem is found, go to Step 2 .
    • If DTC B1175 is indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
  1. Check the No. 21 METER (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box before testing.
  1. Do the gauge drive circuit check.
    • If the fuel gauge needle sweeps from the minimum to maximum position and then returns to minimum, the gauge is OK. Go to Step 3 .
    • If the fuel gauge needle does not sweep correctly, replace the gauge assembly and retest.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Fold the rear seat and rise the seat cushion.
  1. Remove the access panel (A) from the floor.
  1. Disconnect the fuel pump 5P connector (B).
  1. Remove the fuel tank unit from the fuel tank.
  1. Measure resistance between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals with the float at E (EMPTY), 1/2 (HALF FULL), and F (FULL) positions.
    If you do not get the following readings, replace the fuel gauge sending unit.
