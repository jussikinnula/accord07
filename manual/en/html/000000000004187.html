DTC Troubleshooting: 31
LKAS Control Unit DTC 31:
 System Structure Error
NOTE: The adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS system malfunction is detected when the control unit of different specification is installed at the following units replacing.
  • LKAS control unit
  • Adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit
  • EPS (LKAS) control unit
  • LKAS camera
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Select ‘‘LKAS'' on the HDS, and check the ECU ID No. 5 from the data list.
Is ‘‘01'' indicated?
YES -
The LKAS control unit is matcing the specification. Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The LKAS control unit is different specification, check the part number and replace the LKAS control unit with the correct specification one. ■
  1. Select ‘‘ACC'' on the HDS, and check the ECU ID No. 3 from the data list.
Is ‘‘04'' indicated?
YES -
The adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit is matcing the specification. Go to Step 5 .
NO -
  • The adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit is different in the specification, check the part number and replace the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit with the correct specification one.
  • After the replacement, do the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit initialization (DTC 100).■
  1. Select ‘‘ACC'' on the HDS, and check the ECU ID No. 7 from the data list.
