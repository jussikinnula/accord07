Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Trunk Area
Without Trunk Side Spacer
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Remove the clips, then remove the spare tyre lid (A).
  1. Detach the clips, and release the hooks (A) by pulling the trunk rear trim panel (B) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the clips (A), and detach the clips (B), then remove the left trunk side trim panel (C) and right trunk side trim panel (D) from the trunk compartment.
  1. If necessary, remove the trunk floor spacer (A) and tie down hooks (B).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
With Trunk Side Spacer
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Remove the clips, then remove the spare tyre lid (A).
  1. Remove the right trunk side spacer mat (A). Remove the clips, then remove the left trunk side spacer (B) and right trunk side spacer (C).
  1. Detach the clips, and release the hooks (A) by pulling the trunk rear trim panel (B) up, then remove it.
  1. Remove the clips (A), and detach the clips (B), then remove the left trunk side trim panel (C) and right trunk side trim panel (D) from the trunk compartment.
  1. If necessary, remove the tie down hooks (E).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
