DTC Troubleshooting: 62
DTC 62:
 Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor Error
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II) and the adaptive cruise control (ACC) switch ON.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 62 indicated?
YES -
Check for loose or poor connections. If the connectors OK, replace the millimeter wave radar unit. After replacement, do the millimeter wave radar unit aiming. ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
