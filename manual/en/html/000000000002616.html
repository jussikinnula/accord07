Front Individual Map Light Replacement
With Sunroof
  1. Carefully pry off the lens (A) with a small screwdriver.
    Front Map Light:
    8 W x 2

  1. Remove the two mounting screws.
  1. Disconnect connectors (B).
  1. Install the light in the reverse order of removal.
Without Sunroof
  1. Carefully pry off the lens (A) with a small screwdriver.
    Front Map Light:
    8 W x 2

  1. Remove the two mounting screws.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector (B) from the housing (C).
  1. Install the light in the reverse order of removal.
