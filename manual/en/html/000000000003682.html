Hood Latch Replacement
  1. Remove the front grille.
  1. Remove the bolts, then remove the hood latch cover (A) from the hood latch (B).
  1. Disconnect the hood latch switch connector (A).
    NOTE: The adaptive cruise control equipment car must work so as not to damage the adaptive cruise control unit.
  1. Remove the bolts, then remove the hood latch (A) from the body.
  1. Disconnect the hood opener cable (B) from the hood latch. Take care not to kink the cable.
  1. Install the latch in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Apply multipurpose grease to each location of the hood latch indicated by arrows.
    • Make sure the hood opener cable is connected properly and hood latch switch connector is plugged in properly (for some models).
    • Adjust the hood latch alignment.
    • Make sure the hood opens properly and locks securely.
