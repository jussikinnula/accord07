DTC Troubleshooting: B1325
DTC B1325:
 Intermittent Wiper Functions But Auto Wiper Does Not Function
  1. Check the crack or damage of the windshield glass.
Is there any crack or damage?
YES -
Replace the windshield glass.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Check the rain sensor installation.
Is the rain sensor installed properly?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Reinstall the rain sensor, and recheck.■
  1. Check the connection of the rain sensor 5P connector.
Is the connector connected properly?
YES -
Replace the rain sensor .■
NO -
Reconnect the 5P connector, and recheck.■
