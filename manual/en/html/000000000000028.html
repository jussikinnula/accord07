Brake Pedal Replacement
  1. Disconnect the brake pedal position switch connector (A).
  1. Remove the lock pin (B) and joint pin (C).
  1. Remove the brake pedal bracket mounting bolt (D) and nuts (E).
  1. Remove the brake pedal with bracket (F).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Perform the brake pedal and brake pedal position switch adjustment.
