Front Grille Replacement
NOTE:
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the front bulkhead cover and front grille.
  1. Remove the clips (A, B, C), then remove the front grille cover (D).
  1. Remove the clips, then remove the front grille (A).Take care not to drop the clips inside the front bumper.
  1. If necessary, remove the clips and screws, then remove the upper grille molding (A), lower grille molding (B), center grille molding (C), emblem base (D), from the front grille cover (E).
  1. Install the front grille in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check the clips for damage or stress-whitening, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
    • Install the hook portions of the front grille into place securely.
