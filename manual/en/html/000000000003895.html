Navigation System Forced Starting of Display
Starting Procedure
Special Tools Required

SCS service connector
 07PAZ-0010100
  1. Connect the SCS service connector (A) to the navigation service check connector (B) located under the dash.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check that the system starts up and then changes to the ‘‘Navi System Link'' screen.
  1. Remove the SCS connector.
    NOTE: If the system fails to display the ‘‘Navi System Link'' screen, refer to no picture is displayed.
