Radiator and Fan Replacement
  1. Drain engine coolant.
  1. Remove the radiator cover.
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose and lower radiator hose from the radiator.
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connectors and radiator fan switch (Low) connector.
  1. Remove the upper bracket cushions, then pull up the radiator.
  1. Remove the fan shroud assemblies and other parts from the radiator.
  1. Install the radiator in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the upper and lower cushions are set securely.
  1. Refill the radiator with expansion tank coolant, and bleed air from cooling system with the heater valve open.
