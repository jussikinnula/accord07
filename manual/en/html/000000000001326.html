Fuel Heater Removal/Installation
LHD model
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the hand primer cover (A).
  1. Remove the hand primer braket (B).
  1. Disconnect the fuel heater 2P connector (A) and fuel temperature sensor 2P connector (B).
  1. Remove the fuel heater bolts (C).
  1. Disconnect the fuel hoses (D).
  1. Install the part in the reveres order of removal.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
RHD model
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Remove the hand primer cover (A).
  1. Remove the hand primer braket (B).
  1. Disconnect the fuel heater 2P connector (A) and fuel temperature sensor 2P connector (B).
  1. Remove the APP sensor wire harness clip (C).
  1. Remove the fuel heater bolts (D).
  1. Disconnect the fuel hoses (E).
  1. Install the part in the reveres order of removal.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
