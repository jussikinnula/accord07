Front Seat Disassembly/Reassembly - 4-way Power
For some models: SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations LHD, SRS component location RHD and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Apply multipurpose grease to the moving portion of the seat track.
  • To prevent wrinkles in the seat cushion cover, stretch the material evenly over the pad.
