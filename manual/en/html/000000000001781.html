Tailgate Support Strut Replacement
NOTE:
  • Have an assistant help you when removing and installing the support strut.
  • Take care not to scratch the body and tailgate.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Remove the pivot bolts (A), then remove the support strut (B).
  1. Install the tailgate hinge side pivot bolts first, next install the body side, and fix the support strut correctly.
