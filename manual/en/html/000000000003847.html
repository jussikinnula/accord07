Rear Bumper Skirt Trim Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the rear bumper and body.
  • Put on gloves to prevent damage.
  1. Remove the rear bumper skirt trim (A) from the rear bumper (B).
    -1.
    Remove the bolts (C, D) and clips (E) from the rear bumper.
    -2.
    Remove the nuts (F), then remove the rear bumper skirt trim.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check the clips for damage or stress-whitening, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and hooks into place securely.
