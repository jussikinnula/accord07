DTC Troubleshooting: 5
DTC 5:
 A Problem in the Mode Control Linkage, Doors, or Motor
  1. Test the mode control motor.
Is the mode control motor OK?
YES -
Substitute a known-good audio-HVAC module, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original printed circuit board.■
NO -
Replace the mode control motor, or repair the linkage and doors.■
