DTC Troubleshooting: 19
LKAS Control Unit DTC 19:
 A Problem with the Temperature Monitoring for the LKAS Camera
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Set the temperature control dial to Max Cool, the mode control switch to Vent and the recirculation control switch to Recirculate.
  1. Turn the A/C switch on and fun switch to Max, and running the air conditioning for 15 minutes or more.
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Does the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for DTCs with HDS.
Is DTC 19 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Do the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the LKAS control unit 26P connector and the LKAS camera 10P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and LKAS camera 10P connector terminal No. 3 and No. 4 individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -

NO -
Go to Step 12 .
