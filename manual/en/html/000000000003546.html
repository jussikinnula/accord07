ATF Level Check
NOTE: Keep all foreign particles out of the transmission.
  1. Warm up the engine to normal operating temperature (the radiator fan comes on).
  1. Park the vehicle on level ground, and turn the engine off.
    NOTE: Check the fluid level within 60âˆ’90 seconds after turning the engine off.
  1. Remove the dipstick (yellow loop) (A) from the ATF dipstick guide pipe, and wipe it with a clean cloth.
  1. Insert the dipstick aligning the cap (B) with the guide tab (C).
  1. Remove the dipstick (A) and check the fluid level. It should be between upper mark (B) and lower mark (C).
  1. If the level is below the lower mark, check for fluid leaks at the transmission, hose and line joints, and cooler lines.
    If the level is an excess, drain the ATF to proper level.
  1. Pour the recommended fluid into the opening (A) of the dipstick guide pipe to bring it to upper mark. Always use genuine Honda ATF-Z1 automatic transmission fluid (ATF). Using a non-Honda ATF can affect shift quality.
  1. Insert the dipstick back into the dipstick guide pipe with aligning the cap with the guide tab on the guide pipe.
