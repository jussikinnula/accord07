DTC Troubleshooting: 20
LKAS Control Unit DTC 20:
 EPS (LKAS) System Malfunction
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine and test-drive the vehicle at 72 km/h (45 mph) or more, and turn the main switch ON.
  1. Drive a car in the LKAS operating condition.
  1. Watch the EPS (LKAS) indicator.
Does the EPS (LKAS) indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for LKAS control unit DTCs with HDS.
Is DTC 20 indicated?
YES -
Do the troubleshooting for the EPS (LKAS) control unit. ■
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC.■
