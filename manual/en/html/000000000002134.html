Transmission Mount Replacement - Upper
  1. Support the engine with a jack and wood block under the oil pan.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the transmission upper mount bracket.
  1. Remove the transmission upper mount (A).
  1. Install the new transmission upper mount, then loosely tighten the new nuts (B), and loosely tighten the new bolt (C).
  1. Install the transmission upper mount bracket (A), and tighten the new bolts, then loosely tighten the mounting bolt (B).
  1. Remove a jack.
  1. Tighten the transmission upper mount/bracket mounting bolts/nuts in the numbered sequence shown.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
