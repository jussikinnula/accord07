A/T 2nd Clutch Transmission Fluid Pressure Switch Replacement
  1. Remove the intake air duct.
  1. Disconnect the connector from the 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch (A), and remove the switch.
  1. Make sure there is no water, oil, dust, or foreign particles inside the connector.
  1. Install the new 2nd clutch transmission fluid pressure switch with a new sealing washer (B), and tighten the switch to the specified torque.
  1. Connect the connector securely.
  1. Install the intake air duct.
