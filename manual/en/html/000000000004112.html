Adaptive Cruise Control/LKAS Symptom Troubleshooting - Adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator (orange) does not come on
Adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator (orange) does not come on
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Watch the following indicators in the gauge control module:
    • MIL
    • Brake system light
    • Charging light
Do the indicators come on?
YES -
Go to the gauge control module self-diagnostic function. ■
NO -
Go to Step 3 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check the No. 21 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Replace the fuse, then recheck.■
  1. Disconnect gauge control module connector (36P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check for voltage between the gauge control module connector (36P) No. 5 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Substitute a known-good gauge control module and recheck. If the problem goes away, replace the original gauge control module. ■
NO -
Repair an open in the wire, if the wire is OK, replace the under-dash fuse/relay box.■
