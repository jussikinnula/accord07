Rear Inner Fender Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the rear inner fender (A).
    -1.
    Remove the bolt (B) from the wheel arch.
    -2.
    On the front of the wheel arch, remove the screws (C) securing the side sill panel (D) and rear inner fender.
    -3.
    On the rear bumper (E), remove the screws (C, F).
    -4.
    From the wheel arch, remove the clips (G, H, I).
    -5.
    Pull the rear inner fender out.
  1. Install the inner fender in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
