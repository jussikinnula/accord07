Cylinder Head Valve, Spring, and Valve Seal Removal
Special Tools Required

Identify the valves and valve springs as they are removed so that each item can be reinstalled in its original position.
  1. Remove the cylinder head .
  1. Using an appropriate-sized socket (A) and plastic mallet (B), lightly tap the valve retainer to loosen the valve cotters.
  1. Install the spring compressor. Compress the spring, and remove the valve cotters.
