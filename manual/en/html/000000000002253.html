Taillight Replacement
Taillights
  1. Open the tailgate.
  1. Remove the covers (A) from the taillight (B).
  1. Remove the two mounting bolts from the taillight.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (C) from the taillight.
    BRAKE/TAILLIGHT:
    21/5 W
    TURN SIGNAL LIGHT:
    21 W

  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
Inner Taillight
  1. Open the tailgate.
  1. Remove the tailgate trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the taillight (B).
  1. Remove the four mounting nuts, then pull out the inner taillight.
    TAILLIGHT:
    5 W
    BACK-UP LIGHT:
    21 W
    REAR FOG LIGHT:
    21 W

  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
