Driveshaft Dynamic Damper Replacement
  1. Remove the inboard joint.
  1. Remove the dynamic damper bands. Be careful not to damage the dynamic damper.
    • If the band is a welded type, cut the boot band.
    • If the band is a double loop type, lift up the band bend, and push it into the clip.
    • If the band is a low profile type, pinch the band using a commercially available boot band pincers.
  1. Wrap the splines on the driveshaft with vinyl tape (A) to prevent damage to the dynamic damper.
  1. Remove the dynamic damper. Be careful not to damage the dynamic damper.
  1. Adjust the position of the new dynamic damper to the figure follow.
  1. Install the double loop band.
  1. Install the inboard joint.
