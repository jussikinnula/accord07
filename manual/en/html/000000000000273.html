Door Lock Actuator Test - With Super Locking
Driver's Door and Right Rear Door With Super Locking
  1. Remove the driver's or right rear door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table. To prevent damage to the actuator, apply battery voltage only momentarily.
  1. If the actuator does not operate as specified, replace it.
Front Passenger's Door and Left Rear Door With Super Locking
  1. Remove the front passenger's or left rear door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 6P connector from the actuator.
  1. Check actuator operation by connecting power and ground according to the table. To prevent damage to the actuator, apply battery voltage only momentarily.
  1. If the actuator does not operate as specified, replace it.
