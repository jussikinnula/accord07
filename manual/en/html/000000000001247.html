Cylinder Head Cover Removal
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the four injectors.
  1. Remove the bolt (A) securing the fuel feed line, and remove two bolts (B) securing the fuel return line.
  1. Remove the turbocharger boost control solenoid (C).
  1. Remove the power steering hose clamp (A) and breather hose (B), then remove two bolts (C) securing the vacuum line.
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
