    -1.
    Using a trim tool, pry the notch (C), then remove the roof rail bar cap (D).
    -2.
    Remove the nuts (E) from the front bracket (F) and the rear bracket (G).
    -3.
    Remove the bolts (H), then remove the roof rail.
    -4.
    Remove the center cover (I), and nuts (J), then remove the center bracket.
  1. Install the roof rail in the reverse order of removal, and make sure the front and rear covers are snapped tightly into place.
