Rear Window Wiper Motor Replacement
  1. Remove the nut (A), the wiper arm (B) and special nut (C).
  1. Open the tailgate and remove the tailgate rear trim panel.
  1. Disconnect the 4P connector (A) from the rear window wiper motor (B).
  1. Remove the three mounting bolts (C), then remove the rear window wiper motor.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Grease the moving parts.
    • Before reinstalling the wiper arms, turn the wiper switch ON, then OFF to return the wiper shafts to the park position.
    • If necessary, replace any damaged clips.
    • Check the rear window wiper motor operation.
