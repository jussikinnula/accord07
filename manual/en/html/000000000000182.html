Glove Box Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations LHD, SRS component locations RHD and the precautions and procedures before performing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Detach the hook (A) of the glove box damper (B), and remove the screws.
  1. While holding the glove box (A), release the glove box stop portion (B) on each side from the dashboard by pushing it inside, then remove the glove box.
  1. From the inside of the dash, gently push out on the front of the dashboard side cover (A) to release the hooks (B), to remove the cover.
  1. Remove the screw, then remove the glove box damper (A).
  1. Install the glove box in the reverse order of removal.
