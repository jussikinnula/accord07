Maintenance Schedule for Normal and Severe Conditions - Except EU
37,000miles(3year)
This Maintenance Schedule outlines the minimum required maintenance that you should perform to ensure the trouble-free operation of the vehicle. Due to regional and climatic differences, some additional service may be required. Please consult the warranty booklet for a more detalied description.
The following items must be used for ‘‘severe conditions'' indicated on schedule, if the vehicle is driven MAINLY under the specified conditions A to E.
  • Engine oil and engine oil filter: A/B/C/D/E
  • Air cleaner element: D/E
  • Transmission fluid: B/D
A: Driving less than 8 km (5 miles) per trip or, in freezing temperatures, driving less than 16 km (10 miles) per trip.
B: Driving in extremely hot over 35 °C (95 °F) conditions.
C: Extensive idling, or long periods of stop-and-go driving.
D: Trailer towing, driving with a loaded roof rack, or driving in mountainous conditions.
E: Driving on muddy, dusty, or de-iced roads.
