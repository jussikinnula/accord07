Front Bumper Fog Cover Replacement
Without Front Fog Lights
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper and body.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. From the front bumper (A) remove the screw (B) and hooks (C), from the front bumper fog cover (D).
  1. Install the front bumper fog cover in reverse order of removal, and note these items: Push the hooks into place securely.
