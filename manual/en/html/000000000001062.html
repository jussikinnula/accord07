Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Rear Shelf Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Seat cushion and seat side bolster
    • Rear pillar trim, without side curtain airbags, with side curtain airbags
    • Rear center seat belt center anchor bolt
  1. Remove the high-mount brake light (A).
    -1.
    From the trunk compartment, disconnect the high-mount brake light bulb socket (B).
    -2.
    Push the high-mount brake light towards the rear window, and gently pry up at the back to release the front hooks (C).
    -3.
    Release the rear hooks (D), then remove the high-mount brake light.
  1. From both sides, pull the rear speaker grille (A) up to detach the clips (B, C), then remove the rear speaker grille.
  1. Remove the rear shelf (A).
    -1.
    Gently pull back the front edge of the rear shelf to detach the clips.
    -2.
    Lift the rear shelf to detach the clips.
    -3.
    Pull back the lid (B) to release the hooks (C), then open the lid.
  1. Pull the rear center seat belt (D) out through the lid opening, and pull the rear seat belts (E) out through the slit (F) in the rear shelf.
  1. Install the shelf in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • When installing the rear shelf, slip the rear seat belt through the slit and the rear center seat belt into the lid opening in the rear shelf.
    • Push the clips and hooks into place securely.
    • Make sure the high-mount brake light bulb socket is connected securely.
