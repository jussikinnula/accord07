Back-up Light Switch Test
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the audio and the navigation system, then write down the frequencies for the audio preset buttons.
    Disconnect the negative (−) cable first, then the positive (+) cable from the battery. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the battery base.
  1. Remove the intake air cover.
  1. Remove the resonator chamber.
  1. Disconnect the back-up light switch connector (A).
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals of the back-up light switch 2P connector. There should be continuity when the shift lever is in reverse.
  1. If necessary, replace the back-up light switch . Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M) to the switch threads, and install the switch on the transmission housing. Tighten the back-up light switch to the specified torque.
  1. Install the resonator chamber.
  1. Install the battery base, then install the ground cable.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the battery. Connect the positive (+) cable first, then the negative (−) cable to the battery.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the audio and navigation system, then enter the customer's audio presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
