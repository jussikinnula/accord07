  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Disconnect the shift lock solenoid connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between No. 1 terminal of the shift lock solenoid connector and body ground.

YES -

  1. Shift the shift lever into the P position, and press the brake pedal. Do not press the accelerator.

YES -
Check the shift lock mechanism. If the mechanism is OK, replace the shift lock solenoid .■
NO -
Check for an open in the wire between PCM connector terminal E2 and shift lock solenoid connector. If the wire is OK, substitute a known-good PCM and recheck.■
