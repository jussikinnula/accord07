A/T Mainshaft 5th Gear Clearance Inspection
K20Z2 Engine Model
  1. Remove the mainshaft transmission housing bearing.
  1. Assemble 41 x 68 mm thrust washer (A), 4th/5th clutch (B), 4th gear collar (C), and transmission housing bearing (D) on the mainshaft (E). Do not install the O-rings during inspection.
  1. Install the idler gear (F) on the mainshaft by a press, then install the conical spring washer (G) and locknut (H).
  1. Tighten the locknut to 29 N·m (3.0 kgf·m, 22 lbf·ft).
  1. Measure the clearance between the mainshaft flange (A) and 41 x 68 mm thrust washer (B) with a feeler gauge (C), in at least three places. Use the average as the actual clearance.
    Standard:
    0.03−0.11 mm (0.001−0.004 in.)

  1. If the clearance is out of standard, remove the 41 x 68 mm thrust washer and measure its thickness.
  1. Select and install a new thrust washer, then recheck.

  1. Disassemble the shaft and gears.
  1. Reinstall the bearing in the transmission housing.
