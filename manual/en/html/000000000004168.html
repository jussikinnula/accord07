DTC Troubleshooting: 5
LKAS Control Unit DTC 5:
 Lost Communication with EPS (LKAS) Control Unit
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II), and wait for 20 seconds or more.
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Does the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with HDS.
Is DTC 5 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Do the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the EPS (LKAS) control unit connector C (13P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between EPS (LKAS) control unit connector C (13P) terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the EPS (LKAS) control unit and LKAS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Connect the EPS (LKAS) control unit C connector (13P).
  1. Disconnect the LKAS control unit 26P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON II).
  1. Measure the voltage between LKAS control unit 26P connector terminal No. 21 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the EPS (LKAS) control unit and LKAS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the EPS (LKAS) control unit connector C (13P).
  1. Check for continuity between LKAS control unit 26P connector terminal No. 8 and No. 21 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the EPS (LKAS) control unit and LKAS control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 17 .
  1. Check for continuity between LKAS control unit 26P connector terminal No. 21 and EPS (LKAS) control unit connector C (13P) terminal No. 3.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 18 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the EPS (LKAS) control unit.■
  1. Check for continuity between LKAS control unit 26P connector terminal No. 8 and EPS (LKAS) control unit connector C (13P) terminal No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 19 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the LKAS control unit and the EPS (LKAS) control unit.■
  1. Check for EPS (LKAS) control unit DTCs with HDS.
Is there DTC?
YES -
Do the troubleshooting for the EPS (LKAS) control unit.
NO -
Check for loose terminals in the EPS (LKAS) control unit connectors, and repair if necessary. If no poor connections are found, replace the EPS (LKAS) control unit.
