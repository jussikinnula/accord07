Hazard Warning Switch Test
  1. Remove the upper panel.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (A) from the hazard warning switch (B).
  1. Push out the hazard warning switch from behind the upper panel.
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the table.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the bulb (C) or the hazard warning switch.
