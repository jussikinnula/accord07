Fuel Filter Water Bleeding
  1. Place a suitable container under the fuel filter to catch up the water.
  1. Connect the hose (A) to water screw (B).
  1. Loosen the water screw, and bleed the water from the fuel filter.
  1. Tighten the water screw securely, and disconnect the hose.
  1. Start the engine, and then check the showing ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display (A).
    • If the ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display is showing, go to Step 6 .
    • If the ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display is not showing, fuel filter water bleeding is complete.
  1. Disconnect the fuel filter water level switch 2P connector, and then recheck the showing ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display.
    • If the ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display is showing, repair short in the wire between the fuel filter water level switch and the gauge assembly.
    • If the ‘‘WATER IN/FUEL FILTER'' in the odometer/outside temperature display is not showing, replace the fuel filter water level switch.
  1. Bleed the fuel system.
