DTC Troubleshooting: P0335 (4)
DTC P0335:
CKP Sensor No Signal
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P0335 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the CKP sensor and the ECM/PCM.â– 
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the CKP sensor 3P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between CKP sensor 3P connector terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the CKP sensor and PGM-FI main relay 1 (FI MAIN), then go to Step 19 .
  1. Measure voltage between CKP sensor 3P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Measure voltage between CKP sensor 3P connector terminals No. 1 and No. 3.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 16 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the CKP sensor and G101, then go to Step 18 .
  1. Measure voltage between ECM/PCM connector terminal A7 and body ground.
Is there about 5 V?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A7) and the CKP sensor, then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Jump the SCS line with the HDS.
  1. Disconnect ECM/PCM connector A (31P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM/PCM connector terminal A7 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between the ECM/PCM (A7) and the CKP sensor, then go to Step 18 .
NO -
Go to Step 25 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the CKP sensor, then go to Step 18 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM with the HDS.
  1. Clear the CKP pattern with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM/PCM idle learn procedure.
  1. Do the CKP pattern learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.

YES -
