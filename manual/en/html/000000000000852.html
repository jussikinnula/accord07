DTC Troubleshooting: P2135 (7)
DTC P2135:
 TP Sensor A/B Incorrect Voltage Correlation
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Do the ETCS TEST in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Is DTC P2135 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time.Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the throttle body and the throttle actuator control module.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the intake air duct from the throttle body.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Visually check the throttle valve operation.
Does the valve move to the fully closed position temporarily?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the throttle actuator control module 16P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between throttle actuator control module 16P connector terminals No. 10 and No. 12.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 13 .
NO -
Substitute a known-good throttle actuator control module, then go to Step 17 and recheck. If DTC P2135 is not indicated, replace the original throttle actuator control module, then go to Step 17 .
  1. Disconnect the throttle body 6P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between throttle actuator control module 16P connector terminals No. 10 and No. 12.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wires between the TPSA line and the TPSB line, then go to Step 17 .
NO -
Go to Step 15 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Replace the throttle body.
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM/PCM with the HDS.
  1. Do the ECM/PCM idle learn procedure.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs in the DTCs MENU with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P2135 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the throttle body and the throttle actuator control module, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
