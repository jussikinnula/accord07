Vacuum Pump Test
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hose (A) and connect the vacuum pump (B) to the hose.
  1. Start the engine and wait 1 minute. The vacuum should be above 93.3 kPa (700 mmHg, 27.6 in.Hg).
    • If the vacuum is OK, the test is complete.
    • If the vacuum is out of specification, replace the vacuum pump.
