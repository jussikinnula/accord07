IMRC Valve Replacement
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Disconnect the ECT sensor connector (A).
  1. Disconnect the MAP sensor/IAT sensor 2 connector (B).
  1. Remove the air intake passage (C).
  1. Disconnect the IMRC position sensor connector (D).
  1. Disconnect the vacuum hoses (E).
  1. Remove the IMRC valve (F).
  1. Install the sensor in the reverse order of removal with a new gaskets (G).
