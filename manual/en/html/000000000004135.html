DTC Troubleshooting: 21
Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Unit DTC 21:
Yaw Rate Sensor Error
NOTE: If DTC 112 is stored at the same time as DTC 21, troubleshoot DTC 112 first, then recheck for DTC 21.
  1. Check for VSA system DTCs with the HDS.
Are any DTCs indicated?
YES -
Troubleshoot the indicated VSA system DTC(s).■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Does the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure. The system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 21 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC.■
  1. Check YAW RATE S with the HDS.
Is about 2.5 V indicated?
YES -
The yaw rate sensor signal is OK, check for loose or poor connections. If OK, replace the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit. ■
NO -
Go to VSA system troubleshooting. ■
