NOTE:
  • The bearing and bearing outer race should be replaced as a set.
  • Inspect and adjust the carrier bearing preload whenever bearing is replaced.
  • Check the bearing for wear and rough rotation. If the bearing is OK, removal is not necessary.
  1. Remove the carrier bearing (A) with a commercially available puller (B), bearing separator (C), and stepped adapter (D).
  1. Install the new bearings with the special tool using the small end and a press until it bottoms. Press the bearing on securely so there is no clearance between the bearing and the differential carrier.
