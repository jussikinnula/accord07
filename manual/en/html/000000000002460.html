DTC Troubleshooting: 104
DTC 104:
 Sensor Cluster
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Disconnect the HDS from the 16P DLC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II) again.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle around a number of corners.
Does the VSA indicator come on, and is DTC 104 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the VSA control unit 47P connector and sensor cluster 6P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between body ground and the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 6, No. 25, No. 29, and No. 31 individually.
Is there 1 V or more?
YES -
Repair short to power in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 6, No. 25, No. 29, and No. 31 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 6 and sensor cluster 6P connector terminal No. 1.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 31 and sensor cluster 6P connector terminal No. 5.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 13 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 29 and sensor cluster 6P connector terminal No. 2.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 14 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
  1. Check for continuity between the VSA control unit 47P connector terminal No. 25 and sensor cluster 6P connector terminal No. 3.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the VSA modulator-control unit and the sensor cluster.■
  1. Substitute a known-good sensor cluster.

  1. Test-drive the vehicle around a number of corners.
