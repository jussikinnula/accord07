Rear Seat Cargo Net Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE:
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Put on gloves to protective your hands.
  1. Open the cover (A), pry out the caps (B, C), then remove the bolts (D) and screws (E) securing the cargo net (F) on the seat-back, and remove it.
  1. Install the cargo net in the reverse order of removal.
