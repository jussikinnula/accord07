Rear License Trim Replacement
  1. Remove the inner taillight, one side.
  1. Detach the clips, release the edge of the rear license trim (A) from the inner taillight (B), then remove the trim. Take care not to scratch the trunk lid.
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Push the clip portions into place securely.
