Rear Skirt Diffuser Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the rear bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the rear bumper.
  1. Remove the rear skirt diffuser (A) from the rear bumper (B).
    -1.
    Remove the nuts (C).
    -2.
    Remove the clips (D) from both sides of the bumper.
    -3.
    Release the clip plates (E) from both sides of the bumper.
    -4.
    Pull the diffuser back along the edge of it near the double-sided adhesive tape (F) to release it from the bumper.
  1. Install the diffuser in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • If the diffuser will be reinstalled, scrape off the remaining double-sided adhesive tape from the diffuser and bumper, then clean the diffuser and bumper surfaces with a sponge dampened in alcohol where adhesive tape will be applied. Apply primer to the diffuser where adhesive tape will be applied, then attach new double-sided adhesive tape to the diffuser.
    • After installing the diffuser, carefully pull the adhesive backing away, and push the diffuser into place securely.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
