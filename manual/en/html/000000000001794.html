Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Rear Side Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Trunk floor lid and both trunk side lids
    • Front cargo floor
    • Rear trim panel
    • Rear floor box
    • Rear door sill trim
    • Rear seat side bolster
  1. Remove the tonneau cover (A).
  1. With premium sound system: Remove the woofer lid (B) by detaching the clips.
  1. Remove the rear floor side box (A).
    -1.
    Left side with premium sound system/both sides without premium sound system: Remove the trunk side lid(s) (B).
    -2.
    Remove the clip (C) by pinching its tabs.
    -3.
    Detach the clip.
    -4.
    Remove the rear floor box from the hook(s) (D) on the rear side trim panel (E).
  1. Detach the upper clip on the holder bracket cover (A).
  1. Remove the rear door opening trim as needed.
  1. Open the convenience hook (A). Insert a flat-tip screwdriver into the hole behind the convenience hook, pry its lower hook (B) back, and then open the convenience hook.
  1. Remove the rear side trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Remove the tie down hooks (B).
    -2.
    Remove the screw (C) and release the clips (D).
    -3.
    Pull the trim panel back to detach the clips (E) and release the hooks (F), then remove the trim panel.
    -4.
    Disconnect the rear accessory power socket connector (G) (right side) and cargo area light connector (H).
  1. Remove the bolts (A) and screw (B), then remove the lid support bracket (C). Detach the clips (D), then remove the rear cargo floor support (E).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • Make sure the trunk compartment light connector and rear accessory power socket connector are plugged in properly.
