DTC Troubleshooting: 11, 13
DTC 11, 13:
 Torque Sensor
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Start the engine.
Does the EPS indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Stop the engine, and verify the DTC.
Is DTC 11 or 13 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Perform the appropriate troubleshooting for the code indicated.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then disconnect EPS control unit connector D (3P).
  1. Check for continuity between EPS control unit connector D (3P) terminal No. 1 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 10 .
