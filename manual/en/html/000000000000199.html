HID Unit Replacement
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft code for the radio and the navigation system, then write down the frequencies for the radio's preset buttons.
  1. Turn the combination light switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the battery negative cable, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the headlight assembly.
  1. Remove the bulb socket from the HID bulb.
  1. Remove the three mounting screws and the HID unit.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After reconnecting the battery, enter the anti-theft code for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
