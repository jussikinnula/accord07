EVAP Two Way Valve Replacement
Except ECONO model
  1. Remove the trunk floor.
  1. Remove the access panel (A).
  1. Disconnect the hoses (B).
  1. Remove the EVAP two way valve (C).
  1. Install the EVAP two way valve in the reverse order of removal.
ECONO model
  1. Disconnect the hoses (A).
  1. Remove the nut (B).
  1. Remove the EVAP two way valve (C).
  1. Install the EVAP two way valve in the reverse order of removal.
