Interior Trim Removal/Installation - Tailgate Area
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the handy remover set, or equivalent when prying components.
  • Take care not to bend or scratch the trim and panels.
  1. Remove the tailgate upper trim (A).
    -1.
    Release the hooks (B) from the left tailgate side trim (C) and right tailgate side trim (D).
    -2.
    Pull the tailgate upper trim back by hand to detach the clips.
  1. Remove the left tailgate side trim (A) and the light tailgate side trim (B).
    -1.
    Pull out the top of the trim to release the pin (C) from the grommet (D) and detach the clip (E).
    -2.
    Release the hooks (F) and tabs (G) from the tailgate lower trim panel (H).
  1. Remove the tailgate lower trim panel (A).
    -1.
    Pry up on the lid (B), remove the self-tapping ET screw (C) using a T25 Torx bit, then remove the tailgate pull pocket (D). If equipped, disconnect the power tailgate close switch connector (E).
    -2.
    Release the clips (F).
    -3.
    Pull put the trim panel to detach the clips (G, H).
  1. Install the trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Replace any damaged clips.
    • If the threads on a pull pocket screw are worn out, use an oversized self-tapping ET screw, P/N 90137-S1A-E01, made specifically for this application.
    • If equipped, make sure the power tailgate close switch connector is plugged in properly, and check the power tailgate operation.
