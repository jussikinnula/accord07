Wiper/Washer Switch (Combination Switch Control Unit) Replacement - KE/KG/KB
Except KQ model
NOTE: The wiper/washer switch built into the combination switch control unit. For the wiper/washer test, refer to the wiper/washer switch input test.
  1. Remove the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the combination light switch 12P connector (A) and dashboard wire harness 8P connector (B) from the wiper/washer switch (C).
  1. Remove the two screws, then slide out the wiper/washer switch.
