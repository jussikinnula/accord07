Automatic Dimming Inside Mirror Test/Replacement
  1. Remove the automatic dimming inside mirror cover.
  1. Disconnect the 7P connector (A) from the automatic inside dimming mirror (B).
  1. Check for continuity between No. 2 terminal and body ground.
    • If there is continuity, go to Step 4 .
    • If there is no continuity, check for:
      • -
      An open in the wire.
      -
      Poor ground (G503 (LHD), G601 (RHD)).
  1. Check for voltage between No. 1 terminal and body ground with the ignition switch ON (II).
    • If there is battery voltage, go to Step 5 .
    • If there is no voltage, check for:
      • -
      Blown No. 21 (7.5 A) fuse in the driver's under-dash fuse/relay box.
      -
      An open in the wire.
  1. Check for voltage between No. 3 terminal and body ground with the ignition switch turned ON (II) and transmission range switch (A/T) or back-up light switch (M/T) in reverse.
    • If there is battery voltage, replace the mirror assembly.
    • If there is no voltage, check for:
      • -
      An open in the wire.
      -
      Blown No. 21 (7.5 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
      -
      Faulty reverse relay.
      -
      Faulty transmission range switch .
