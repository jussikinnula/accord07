DTC Troubleshooting: B1352
DTC B1352:
 Power Tailgate Control Unit Internal Error
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Remove the No. 7 (10 A) fuse from the under-dash fuse/relay box, and reinstall it after 1 minute.
  1. Open the tailgate fully with the transmitter.
  1. Close the tailgate fully with the manual operation. Do not close the tailgate with the power tailgate inner switch or the transmitter.
  1. Check the DTCs by selecting the DTCs MENU (DTCs) from the HDS.
Is DTC B1352 indicated?
YES -
Faulty power tailgater control unit; replace it.■
NO -
Intermittent failure the power tailgate control unit is OK at this time.■
