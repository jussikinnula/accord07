Starter Replacement
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and navigation system, then write down the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the expansion tank (A), then move the expansion tank.
  1. Remove the dipstick/tube assembly (A) and O-ring (B).
  1. Remove the bolt securing the harness holder.
  1. Remove the harness clamps (A), then disconnect the starter cable (B) from the B terminal and BLK/WHT wire (C) from the S terminal.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the starter, then remove the starter.
  1. Install the starter in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the crimped side of the ring terminal (A) is facing out.
  1. Check the starter through bolt for tightness.
    Specified Torque:
    11 N·m (1.1 kgf·m, 8.0 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the bolt securing the harness holder.
  1. Install the dipstick/tube assembly with new O-ring.
  1. Install the expansion tank.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, then connect the negative cable.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
  1. Enter the anti-theft code for the radio and the navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Set the clock.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
