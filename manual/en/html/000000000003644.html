ABS Indicator Circuit Troubleshooting - Without VSA
ABS indicator does not come on
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and watch the ABS indicator.
Does the ABS indicator come on for several seconds?
YES -
The system is OK at this time.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Do the gauge control module self-diagnosis function procedure.
Is gauge control module OK?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Replace gauge control module. ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the gauge control module.
  1. Disconnect the gauge control module connector (36P).
  1. Cheek for continuity between gauge control module connector (36P) terminal No. 13 and the body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short to body ground in the wire between the gauge control module and the ABS modulator-control unit.■
NO -
Check for loose terminals in the gauge control module connectors. If connectors are OK, replace the gauge control module .■
ABS indicator does not go off, and no DTCs are stored
  1. Disconnect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector terminal No. 16 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the No. 18 (15 A) fuse and the ABS modulator-control unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector terminal No. 24 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between the ABS modulator-control unit and body ground (G203).■
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Connect the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector terminal No. 21 and body ground with a jumper wire.
Does the ABS indicator go off?
YES -
Check for the loose terminals in the ABS modulator-control unit 25P connector. If necessary, substitute a known-good ABS modulator-control unit, and recheck.■
NO -
Go to Step 8 .
  1. Remove the gauge control module, and leave the connectors connected.
  1. Connect gauge control module connector (36P) terminal No. 13 and body ground with a jumper wire.
Does the ABS indicator go off?
YES -
Repair open in the wire between the gauge control module and the ABS modulator-control unit.■
NO -
Check for loose terminals in the gauge control module connectors. If the connectors are OK, replace the gauge control module. ■
