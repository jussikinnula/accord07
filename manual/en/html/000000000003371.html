MTF Inspection and Replacement
  1. Park the vehicle on level ground, and turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the oil check bolt (A) and washer (B), check the condition of the fluid. If the transmission fluid is dirty, remove the drain plug (C) and washer (D).
  1. Install the drain plug with a new washer.
  1. Make sure you have the anti-theft codes for the radio and the navigation system, then write down the frequencies for the radio preset buttons.
    Disconnect the negative (−) cable first, then the positive (+) cable from the battery. Remove the battery.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the battery base.
  1. Remove the intake air cover.
  1. Remove the resonator chamber.
  1. Remove the oil filler plug (A) and washer (B).
  1. Refill the transmission fluid from the oil filler plug hole of transmission, and until the transmission fluid overflows from the oil check bolt hole (A).
  1. After it stops overflowing, install the oil check bolt (B) with a new washer (C).

    Fluid Capacity

    2.2 L (2.3 US qt, 1.9 Imp qt) at Fluid Change
    2.5 L (2.6 US qt, 2.2 Imp qt) at Overhaul

    Always use Honda Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF). Using motor oil can cause stiffer shifting because it does not contain the proper additives.
  1. Install the oil filler plug (A) with a new washer (B).
  1. Install the resonator chamber.
  1. Install the intake air cover.
  1. Install the battery base, then install the ground cable.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the battery. Connect the positive (+) cable first, then the negative (−) cable to the battery.
  1. Enter the anti-theft codes for the radio and navigation system, then enter the customer's radio station presets.
  1. Do the power window control unit reset procedure.
  1. Install the splash shield.
