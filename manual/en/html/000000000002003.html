ECM Replacement
  1. Connect the HDS to the DLC (A) located under the driver's side of the dashboard.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Select READ DATA in the REPLACE ECM menu with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and wait 30 seconds.
  1. Remove the passenger's kick panel.
  1. Disconnect ECM connectors (A).
  1. Loosen the bolts (B).
  1. Remove the bolts (C), then remove the ECM (D).
  1. Install the ECM in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Select WRITE DATA in the REPLACE ECM menu with the HDS.
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
