LKAS Camera Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • When removing the LKAS camera, take care not to touch the lens of the LKAS camera.
  • Before installation, check if the lens of the LKAS camera is clean. If it is dirty, wipe the lens off with a soft towel dampened with alcohol.
  1. Remove the roof console (A).
  1. Remove the LKAS camera cover (A), then remove the three mounting bolts.
    NOTE: When removing the LKAS camera cover, push the hooks (B) using the screwdriver and pull down the LKAS camera cover, then lift the front side of cover up and remove the LKAS camera cover.
  1. Disconnect 10P connector (C) and remove the LKAS camera (D).
  1. Install the LKAS camera in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After installation, do the LKAS camera adjustment (aiming).
