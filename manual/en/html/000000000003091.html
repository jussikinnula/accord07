DTC Troubleshooting: P1030 (183-1)
DTC P1030:
 Fuel Heater Circuit Malfunction
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Do the FUEL HEATER ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P1030 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Check for poor connections or loose terminals at the fuel heater relay and the ECM.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check No. 2 (7.5 A) fuse in the multi fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Replace No. 2 (7.5 A) fuse, then go to Step 15 .
  1. Check No. 11 +B PTC (FUEL HEATER) (30 A) fuse in the under-hood fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
  • Repair short in the wire between No. 11 +B PTC (FUEL HEATER) (30 A) fuse and the fuel heater relay. Also replace No. 11 +B PTC (FUEL HEATER) (30 A) fuse, then go to Step 15 .
  • Repair short in the wire between the fuel heater relay and No. 2 (7.5 A) fuse in the multi fuse/relay box, then go to Step 15 .
  1. Disconnect the fuel heater relay.
  1. Check the fuel heater relay.
Is the fuel heater relay OK?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Replace the fuel heater relay, then go to Step 14 .
  1. Measure voltage between fuel heater relay 4P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire between No. 11 +B PTC (FUEL HEATER) (30 A) fuse and the fuel heater relay, then go to Step 14 .
  1. Disconnect the fuel heater 2P connector.
  1. Disconnect ECM connector No. 1 (96P).
  1. Check for continuity between ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 37 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair short in the wire between No. 2 (7.5 A) fuse and ECM connector No. 1 terminal No. 37. Also replace No. 2 (7.5 A) fuse in the multi fuse/relay box, then go to Step 14 .
NO -
Go to Step 19 .
  1. Reconnect all connectors.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Reset the ECM with the HDS.
  1. Do the FUEL HEATER ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P1030 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the fuel heater relay and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
Troubleshooting is complete.■
  1. Update the ECM if it does not have the latest software, or substitute a known-good ECM.
  1. Do the FUEL HEATER ON/OFF in the INSPECTION MENU with the HDS.
  1. Check for Temporary DTCs or DTCs with the HDS.
Are any Temporary DTCs or DTCs indicated?
YES -
If DTC P1030 is indicated, check for poor connections or loose terminals at the fuel heater relay and the ECM, then go to Step 1 . If any other Temporary DTCs or DTCs are indicated, go to the indicated DTC's troubleshooting.
NO -
If the ECM was updated, troubleshooting is complete. If the ECM was substituted, replace the original ECM. ■
