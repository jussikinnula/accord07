Rear Pillar Outer Trim Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the taillight.
  1. While pulling the rear pillar outer trim (A), cut through the double-sided adhesive tape (B, C) with a utility knife, then remove it.

    Adhesive tape

    B:
      Thickness 1.6 mm (0.063 in.)
      Width 10 mm (0.39 in.)
    C:
      Thickness 2.5 mm (0.098 in.)
      Width 10 mm (0.39 in.)

  1. Install the rear pillar outer trim in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the trim is to be reinstalled, scrape off the remaining double-sided adhesive tape from the trim and body, then clean the trim and body surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol where adhesive tape will be applied. Attach the upper double-sided adhesive tape (B) and another double-sided adhesive tape (C) to the original position in the trim.
    • Fit the pins (D) to the clips (E) on the rear pillar, then push the clips and adhesive portions into place securely.
    • Replace any damaged clips with new ones.
