Fuel Pressure Regulator Replacement
Except ECONO model
  1. Remove the fuel pump.
  1. Remove the clip (A).
  1. Remove the fuel pressure regulator (B).
  1. Install the regulator in the reverse order of removal with new O-rings (C).
    Make sure the regulator is installed with the drain hole (D) of facing down.
ECONO model
  1. Remove the fuel pump.
  1. Remove the holder (A).
  1. Remove the fuel pressure regulator (B).
  1. Install the regulator in the reverse order of removal with new O-ring (C).
    NOTE: Coat the O-ring with clean engine oil.
