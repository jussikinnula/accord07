Engine Oil Replacement
  1. Warm up the engine.
  1. Remove the maintenance lid (A).
  1. Remove the drain bolt (B), and drain the engine oil.
  1. Reinstall the drain bolt with a new washer (C).
  1. Refill with the recommended oil.
  1. Clean up any spilled engine oil.
  1. Run the engine for more than 3 minutes, then check for oil leakage.
    Capacity
    At Oil Change:
    5.5 L (5.8 US qt, 4.8 Imp qt)
    At Oil Change including Filter:
    5.9 L (6.2 US qt, 5.2 Imp qt)
    After Engine Overhaul
    6.5 L (6.9 US qt, 5.7 Imp qt)
