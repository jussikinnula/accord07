Parking Brake Shoe Lining Break-in
Perform this operation in a safe area.

NOTE: Perform brake linings surface break-in when replacing shoes with new brake linings and/or new rear brake disc/drum.
  1. Park the vehicle on a firm, level surface.
  1. Do the major parking brake adjustment.
  1. Do the minor parking brake adjustment.
  1. Pull the parking brake lever 88 N (9 kgf, 19.8 lbf) of force while driving the vehicle for 400 m (1/4 mile).
  1. Drive the vehicle for 400 m (1/4 mile) at 50 km/h (31 mph).
  1. Stop the vehicle and release the parking brake for 5âˆ’10 minutes to allow the brake disc/drum to cool.
  1. Repeat steps 4 through 6 three more times.
  1. Check the parking brake lever adjustment.
