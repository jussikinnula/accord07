Trunk Lid Opener/Fuel Fill Door Opener Replacement
NOTE:
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Using a handy remover, detach the hooks (A) by prying the front side cap (B), then remove it from the front door sill trim (C), and if equipped, remove the opener lock cylinder (D).
  1. Remove the bolt (E).
  1. Remove the front door sill trim.
  1. Remove the screw, then remove the trunk lid opener/fuel fill door opener (A) from the front door sill trim (B).
  1. Install the opener in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Make sure the opener cable is connected properly.
    • Make sure the trunk lid and fuel fill door open properly and lock securely.
