Front Grille Replacement
NOTE:
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • Take care not to scratch the front bulkhead cover and front grille.
  1. Remove the clips (A, B, C, D), then remove the front grille cover (E).
  1. Remove the clips, then remove the front grille (A). Take care not to drop the clips inside the front bumper.
  1. If necessary, remove the screws, then remove the upper grille molding (A), lower grille molding (B), center grille molding (C), center grille base (D), emblem (E), emblem base (F), air guide grille (G), front grille molding (H), from the front grille cover (I).
  1. Install the front grille in the reverse order of removal, and check the clips for damage or stress-whitening, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
