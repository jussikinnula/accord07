Vanity Mirror Light Test
  1. Open the sunvisor.
  1. Remove the sunvisor.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (A) from the vanity mirror light.
    Vanity mirror light:
    2 W

  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals.
    • With the vanity mirror cover (B) opened, there should be continuity.
    • With the vanity mirror cover closed, there should be no continuity.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the bulb (C) or the sunvisor.
