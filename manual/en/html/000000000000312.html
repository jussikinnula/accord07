Power Window Passenger's Switch Test/Replacement
  1. Carefully pry off the door switch trim.
  1. Disconnect the power window switch connector (A), then remove the three mounting screws and the power window switch (B) from the switch trim (C).
  1. Swap the window switch with another known-good switch and test.
    If the original window switch is faulty, replace it.
