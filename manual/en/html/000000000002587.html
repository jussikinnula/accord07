Radio Remote Switch Test - Without Cruise Control
Without cruise control ('05 Model)
  1. Remove the driver's airbag assembly.
  1. Remove the 5P connector from the cable reel.
  1. Measure resistance between the No. 2 and No. 3 terminals in each switch position according to the table.
    Position
    Resistance
    OFF
    About 10 kΩ
    MODE
    About 6 kΩ
    CH (+)
    About 2 kΩ
    CH (−)
    About 840 Ω
    ▲
    (VOL. UP)
    About 370 Ω
    ▼
    (VOL. DOWN)
    About 100 Ω

  1. Check the continuity between the No. 3 and No. 4 terminals in each switch position according to the table.
  1. If the resistance is not as specified, replace the remote switch.
