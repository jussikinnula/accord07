Front Bumper Air Spoiler Replacement - Sports
Sports
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the screws (A) from the rear edge of the front bumper air spoiler (B).
  1. Remove the bolts (C) from under the front bumper air spoiler.
  1. Pull the front bumper air spoiler back along the edge of it near the double-sided adhesive tapes (D, E, F) to release them from the front bumper (G), then remove the spoiler.
  1. Release the hooks (H) of the cover (I) from the front bumper air spoiler.
  1. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the spoiler is to be reinstalled, scrape off the remaining double-sided adhesive tape from the spoiler and bumper, then clean the spoiler and bumper surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol where adhesive tape will be applied. Apply primer to the spoiler where adhesive tape will be applied, then attach new double-sided adhesive tapes to the spoiler.
    • After installing the spoiler, carefully pull the adhesive backing away, and push the spoiler into place securely.
