Dust and Pollen Filter Replacement
NOTE: LHD type is shown, RHD type is symmetrical.
The dust and pollen filter should be replaced every 30,000 km (18,750 miles) or 1 year, whichever comes first. Replace the filter more often if the air flow is less than usual.
  1. Open the glove box. Remove the damper from the glove box, then let the glove box hang down.
  1. Remove the dust and pollen filter assembly from the evaporator.
  1. Remove the filter (A) from the housing (B), and replace the filter.
  1. Install the filter in the reverse order of removal.
    Make sure that there is no air leaking out of the evaporator.
