Crankshaft Pulley Removal and Installation
Special Tools Required

Handle
 07JAB-001020B

Pulley holder attachment, HEX 50 mm
 07JAB-0010400

Socket wrench, 19 mm
 07JAA-001020A
Removal
  1. Remove front tyres/wheels.
  1. Remove the splash shield.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Hold the pulley with handle (A) and holder attachment (B).
  1. Remove the bolt with a 19 mm socket (C) and breaker bar.
Installation
  1. Clean the crankshaft pulley (A), crankshaft (B), bolt (C), and washer (D). Lubricate as shown.
  1. Install the crankshaft pulley, and hold the pulley with handle (A) and holder attachment (B).
  1. Tighten the bolt to 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft) with a torque wrench and 19 mm socket (C). Do not use an impact wrench.
  1. Tighten the pulley bolt an additional 90 °.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Install the splash shield.
  1. Install front tyres/wheels.
