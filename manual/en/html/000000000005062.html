Combination Light Switch Test/Replacement
LHD type
  1. Remove the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Remove the steering column covers.
  1. Disconnect the 12P connector (A) from the wiper/washer switch (B).
  1. Remove the two screws, then slide out the combination light switch (A).
  1. Inspect the connector terminals to be sure they are all making good contact.
    • If the terminals are bent, loose or corroded, repair them as necessary, and recheck the system.
    • If the terminals look OK, check for continuity between the terminals in each switch position according to the tables.
      • -
      If there is no continuity replace switch.
