DTC Troubleshooting: B1156
DTC B1156:
 Gauge Control Module Lost Communication (Wiper Switch Message) with the Combination Switch Control Unit
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Cycle the ignition switch to OFF, and then back ON.
  1. Check the DTCs by selecting the DTCs MENU (DTCs) from the HDS.
Is DTC B1156 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure. The gauge control system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check the DTCs by selecting the DTCs MENU (DTCs) of the HDS.
Is DTC B1009 or B1063 indicated?
YES -
Go to combination switch control unit input test. ■
NO -
Go to gauge control module input test. ■
