Front Bumper Air Spoiler Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the front bumper air spoiler (A).
  1. Pull the front bumper air spoiler back to detach the hooks (B), and then remove the spoiler.
  1. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal.
