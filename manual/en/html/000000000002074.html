Rear Suspension Knuckle Bushing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Bushing driver 30−40
 07NAD-SS00100

or 07NAD-SS00101
  1. Position the knuckle (A) on the press with the machined surface (B) facing down.
  1. Adjust the special tool so it matches the inner diameter of the bushing hole, then tighten the socket bolts (C) securely.
  1. Position the special tool on the bushing (D).
  1. Remove the bushing by pressing on the special tool with a press as shown.
    NOTE: Be careful not to damage the inside of the bushing hole while pressing on the bushing.
  1. Position the knuckle (A) on the press with the machined surface (B) facing up.
  1. Adjust the special tool so it matches with the outer diameter of the bushing (C).
  1. Position the special tool on the outer sleeve (D) of the bushing.
  1. Press the bushing into the knuckle using the special tool as shown.
