Electrical Power Steering Rack End Removal and Installation

Exproded view
Removal
Note these items during removal/installation:
  • Do not allow dust, dirt, or other foreign materials to enter the gearbox.
  • Do not disassemble the steering gearbox assembly (motor). If the motor is faulty, replace it as an assembly.
  1. Remove the boot bands (A) and tie-rod clips (B). Pull the boots away from the right and left ends of the steering gearbox.
  1. Unbend the lock washers (A) on both sides of the strreing gearbox.
  1. Unscrew both rack ends (A) with a wrench. Be careful not to damage the rack shaft surface with the wrench. Remove the rack end, lock washer (B) and stop rubber (C) discard them.
Installation
  1. Install a new stop rubber (A) and lock washer (B) on the rack shaft. Align the lock washer tabs (C) with slots (D) in the rack shaft. Install the rack end (E) while holding with lock washer in place. Repeat this step for the other side of the rack shaft.
  1. Tighten both rack ends. Be careful not to damage the rack shaft surface with the wrench.
  1. Bend the lock washer back against the flat spots on the rack end ball joint housing.
  1. Apply multipurpose grease to the circumference (A) of the rack end joint housing and lock washer.
  1. Apply a light coat of silicone grease to the boot fitting grooves (B) on the rack ends.
  1. Center the steering rack within its stroke.
  1. Clean off the any grease or contamination from the boot installation grooves (A) around on the gearbox housing. Install the boots (B) on the rack ends with the tie-rod clips (C), and fit the boot end in the installation grooves in the housing properly.
  1. After installing the boots, wipe the grease off the thread section (D) of rack end.
  1. Install the new boot bands by aligning the tabs (A) with holes (B) of the band.
  1. Close the ear portion (A) of the band (B) with commercially available pincers, Oetiker 1098 or equivalent (C).
  1. Slide the rack shaft right and left to be certain that the boots are not deformed or twisted.
