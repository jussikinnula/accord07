Dashboard Side Vent Removal/Installation
Driver's
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the driver's dashboard lower cover.
  1. Push up the lower clips by hand, then push out the driver's side vent (A) by releasing the upper and side tabs (B).
  1. Reinstall the tab portions of the vent first, then push the clip portions into place securely.
Passenger's
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Detach the hook of the glove box damper, then lower the glove box.
  1. Carefully insert a flat-tip screwdriver under the clip, and push it up.
  1. Pull out along the edge of the passenger's side vent (A) by hand to release the lower clips and the upper and side tabs (B). Then remove the vent.
  1. Reinstall the tab portions of the vent first, then push the clip portions into place securely.
