A/C Compressor Relief Valve Replacement
  1. Recover the refrigerant with a recovery/recycling/charging station.
  1. Remove the relief valve (A), and the O-ring (B). Plug the opening to keep foreign matter from entering the system and the A/C compressor oil from running out.
  1. Clean the mating surfaces.
  1. Install a new O-ring on the relief valve, and apply a thin coat of refrigerant oil to the O-ring.
  1. Remove the plug, and install and tighten the relief valve.
  1. Charge the system.
