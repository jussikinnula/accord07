Power Tailgate Drive Unit Replacement
  1. Remove these items:
    • Center pillar upper trim
    • Quarter pillar trim
    • Roof end trim
    • Grab handle
  1. Headliner is lowered.
  1. Remove the three mounting bolts from the power tailgate control unit.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A) from the power tailgate control unit (B).
  1. Remove the lock washer (A) and washer (B) from the tailgate hinge (C).
  1. Remove the four mounting bolts, then remove the power tailgate drive unit (D).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
