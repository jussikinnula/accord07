Dashboard Driver's Lower Cover Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • When prying components, use the handy remover set or equivalent.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Adjust the steering column upward.
  1. Remove the screw (A) securing the dashboard lower cover (B). Starting at the left lower corner working toward the right, gently pull out the bottom to detach four clips (C) and a hook (D). Disconnect the VSA switch connector (E), and if equipped, headlight adjuster switch connector (F).
  1. While pulling the left of the dashboard lower cover (A) back, pry the clips (B, C) nearest the center console with a flat-tip screwdriver, then remove the cover.
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
