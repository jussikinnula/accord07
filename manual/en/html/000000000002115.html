VSA Sensor Cluster Replacement
NOTE:
  • Do not damage or drop the sensor as it is sensitive.
  • LHD type is shown, RHD type is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the driver's/passenger's center lower cover and rear vent duct.
  1. Remove the bracket mounting bolt (A) on driver's side.
  1. Remove the bracket mounting bolt (A) on passenger's side.
  1. Remove the connector mounting bracket (B).
  1. Remove the sensor cluster (C) with the bracket.
  1. Disconnect the sensor cluster connector (A).
  1. Remove the bracket (B) from the sensor cluster (C).
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
