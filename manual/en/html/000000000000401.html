DTC Troubleshooting: 33-3x
DTC 33-3x (33-30 to 33-39, 33-3A to 33-3F):
Short to Another Wire or Decreased Resistance in Left Side Curtain Airbag Inflator
Special Tools Required

SRS inflator simulator
 07SAZ-TB4011A

SRS simulator lead E
 07XAZ-S1A0200

SRS short canceller
 070AZ-SAA0100
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check that the SRS indicator comes on for about 6 seconds and then goes off.
Does the SRS indicator stay on and is DTC 33-3x indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, system is OK at this time. Go to Troubleshooting Intermittent Failures. If another DTC is indicated, go to the DTC troubleshooting index for troubleshooting details.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect the left side curtain airbag 2P connector from the side curtain airbag subharness (A).
  1. Connect the SRS inflator simulator (2 Ω connector) and simulator lead E to the side curtain airbag subharness.
  1. Reconnect the battery negative cable.
  1. Erase the DTC memory.
  1. Read the DTC.
Is DTC 33-3x indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 9 .
NO -
Short to another wire in the left side curtain airbag inflator; replace the left side curtain airbag.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF. Disconnect the battery negative cable, and wait for 3 minutes.
  1. Disconnect SRS unit connector C (16P) from the SRS unit.
  1. Disconnect the SRS inflator simulator from SRS simulator lead.
  1. Connect the SRS short canceller (070AZ-SAA0100) to No. 11 and No. 12 terminals of SRS unit connector C (16P).
  1. Check resistance between the terminals of the SRS simulator lead. There should be an open circuit or at least 1 MΩ.
Is the resistance as specified?
YES -
Faulty SRS unit; replace the SRS unit. ■
NO -
Short to another wire in the side curtain airbag subharness; replace the side curtain airbag subharness.■
