Emblem/Sticker Replacement
NOTE: When removing the emblems/sticker, take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Clean the body surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol. After cleaning, keep oil, grease, and water from getting on the surface.
  1. Apply the emblem/sticker where shown. When installing the rear i-VTEC sticker on the rear window, align the application tape with the edge of the rear window, and center it, then press the sticker into place, and remove the application tape.
