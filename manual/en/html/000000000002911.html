Front Air Spoiler Replacement - Type-S
Type-S
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the screws (A) from the rear edge of the front air spoiler (B).
  1. Remove the bolts (C, D) and clips (E) from under the front air spoiler and the brackets (F).
  1. Pull the front air spoiler back along the edge of it near the double-sided adhesive tapes (G, H, I) to release them from the front bumper (J), then remove the spoiler.
  1. Install the spoiler in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the spoiler is to be reinstalled, scrape off the remaining double-sided adhesive tape from the spoiler and bumper, then clean the spoiler and bumper surface with a sponge dampened in alcohol where adhesive tape will be applied. Apply primer to the spoiler where adhesive tape will be applied, then attach new double-sided adhesive tapes (G, H, I) to the spoiler.
    • After installing the spoiler, carefully pull the adhesive backing away, and push the spoiler into place securely.
