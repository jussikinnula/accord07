DTC Troubleshooting: 61
Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Unit DTC 61:
Millimeter Wave Radar Unit Communication Error
  1. Clear the DTC with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Watch the adaptive cruise control (ACC)/LKAS indicator.
Do the indicator come on?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC 61 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Troubleshoot the indicated DTC(s). If there are no DTCs, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check the No. 13 (20 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Replace the fuse and recheck.■
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector from the millimeter wave radar unit.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check for voltage between the millimeter wave radar unit 5P connector No. 1 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire between the millimeter wave radar unit and the No. 13 (20 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for continuity between the millimeter wave radar unit 5P connector No. 4 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Go to Step 12 .
NO -
Repair an open in the wire or poor ground (G203).■
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II) and main switch ON.
  1. Check for voltage between the millimeter wave radar unit 5P connector No. 5 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 15 .
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Check for voltage between the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit 20P connector No. 17 terminal and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair an open in the wire between the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit and the millimeter wave radar unit.■
NO -
Check for loose or poor connections. If OK, replace the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit , then go to Step 18 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the 20P connector from the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit.
  1. Check for continuity between the millimeter wave radar unit 5P connector No. 5 terminal and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair a short to ground in the wire between the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit and the millimeter wave radar unit.■
NO -
Check for loose or poor connections. If OK, replace the adaptive cruise control (ACC) unit , then go to Step 18 .
