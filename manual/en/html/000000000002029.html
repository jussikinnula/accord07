Fuel Pump Replacement
Removal
  1. Remove the engine cover.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Disconnect the fuel pump connector (A).
  1. Remove the high pressure pipe (B).
  1. Disconnect the fuel hoses (C).
  1. Remove the fuel pump (D).
Installation
  1. Tighten the nuts (A) by the hand with a new high pressure pipe (B).
  1. Install the fuel pump (C).
    NOTE: When installing the fuel pump, set the fuel pump and camshaft (D).
  1. Tighten the nut of fuel pump side (E).
  1. Tighten the nut of fuel rail side (A).
  1. Install the high pressure pipe clamp and rubber (F).
    NOTE: If the high pressure pipe clamp and rubber are not installed, fuel may leak from fuel pipe.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal, and bleed the fuel system.
