DTC Troubleshooting: B1376
DTC B1376:
 Power Tailgate Outer Handle Switch Signal Error
  1. Clear the DTCs using the HDS.
  1. Cycle the ignition switch to OFF, and then back ON.
  1. After 2 secons or more, pull the tailgate outer handle 2 seconds or more.
  1. Check for DTCs by the DTCs MENU (DTCs) from the HDS.
Is DTC B1376 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Cycle the ignition swith to OFF.
  1. Open the tailgate fully with the transmitter.
  1. Disconnect connector A (26P) from the power tailgate control unit.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 10 and No. 12 terminals of power tailgate control unit connector A (26P) and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair a short in the WHT/RED (OHSW) or YEL/BLK (SCOM) wire.■
NO -
Go to Step 9 .
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 10 and No. 12 terminals of power tailgate control unit connector A (26P).
Is there continuity with tailgate outer handle pulled, and no continuity with tailgate outer handle released?
YES -
Faulty power tailgate control unit; replace it.■
NO -
Repair an open in the WHT/RED (OHSW) or YEL/BLK (SCOM) wire, or faulty power tailgate outer handle switch: replace it.■
