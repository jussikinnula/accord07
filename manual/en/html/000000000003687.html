Gauge Control Module Replacement
  1. Remove the instrument panel , then remove the upper column cover.
  1. Place a clean shop towel (A) under the gauge control module to prevent scratching the steering column or dash panel.
  1. Remove the screws from the gauge control module (B).
  1. Disconnect the connector (C), and remove the gauge control module.
  1. Install the gauge control module in the reverse order of removal.
