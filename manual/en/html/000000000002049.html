Engine Oil Filter Replacement
  1. Remove the oil filter assembly using the oil filter wrench (A) (P/N 04151-RBD-305 or equivalent). Use only socket type oil filter wrench to avoid damage to the oil filter cap.
  1. Separate the oil filter cap and oil filter element.
  1. Install the new O-rings (A) and oil filter element (B) to the oil filter cap (C).
  1. Apply new engine oil around the O-rings, then install the oil filter assembly to the oil filter housing.
  1. Tighten the oil filter assembly using the oil filter wrench (P/N 04151-RBD-305 or equivalent).
    Specified Torque: 25 N·m (2.5 kgf·m, 18 lbf·ft)
