DTC 12, 14, 16, 18:
 Wheel Sensor (Electrical Noise/Intermittent Interruption)
NOTE: If the ABS indicator comes on because of electrical noise, the indicator goes off when you drive the vehicle over 30 km/h (19 mph) and the noise is gone.
