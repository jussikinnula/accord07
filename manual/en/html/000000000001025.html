Rear Seat Belt Replacement
NOTE: Check the rear seat belts for damage, and replace them if necessary. Be careful not to damage them during removal and installation.
Rear Seat Belt
  1. Remove the rear seat cushion.
  1. Remove the lower anchor bolt (A).
  1. Remove the rear shelf.
  1. Remove the retractor mounting bolt (A) and the retractor bolt (B), then remove the rear seat belt (C) and retractor (D).
  1. Install the seat belt in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions as described.
    • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten to specification with a torque wrench.
    • Before installing the anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the rear seat belt.
Center Seat Belt and Seat Belt Buckles
  1. Remove the rear seat cushion.
  1. Remove the center anchor bolts (A), and remove the seat belt buckles (B).
  1. Remove the rear shelf.
  1. Remove the retractor mounting self-tapping ET screw (A) and the retractor bolt (B), then remove the center seat belt (C) and retractor (D).
  1. Remove the protector (A).
  1. Install the seat belt and buckles in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check that the retractor locking mechanism functions.
    • Assemble the washers on the center anchor bolt as shown.
    • Tighten the bolts by hand first, then tighten to specification with a torque wrench.
    • Before installing the center anchor bolt, make sure there are no twists or kinks in the center belt.
