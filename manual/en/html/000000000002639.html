Navigation System Symptom Troubleshooting - The picture is missing a Red, Green or Blue color or tone
The picture is missing a Red, Green or Blue color or tone
NOTE: Always check the connectors for poor connections or loose terminals.
  1. Perform the start-up procedure. Press the monitor button then press the RGB color button.
Are the Red, Green, and Blue colored circles shown?
YES -
Push the return button. Push the color change button, and then press the Default button to restore the standard color settings.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to OFF.
  1. Disconnect the navigation unit connector A (20P) and display unit 20P connector.
  1. Check for loose terminals at navigation unit connector A (20P) and display unit 20P connector.
Are there loose terminals?
YES -
Repair the terminal.■
NO -
Go to Step 5 .
  1. Check for continuity between the appropriate terminals of navigation unit connector A (20P) to display unit 20P connector based on the missing color(s).
