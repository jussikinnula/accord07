Rear Seat Armrest Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  1. Remove the clip (A) from the left portion of the armrest pivot.
  1. Remove the armrest (A).
    -1.
    Slide the armrest toward the driver's side of the vehicle.
    -2.
    Remove the pivot shaft (B) from the collar (C) on the passenger's side of the vehicle by pulling up on the armrest, and remove the pivot shaft from the collar (D) by pulling up on the armrest.
  1. Remove the beverage holder.
  1. Install the armrest in the reverse order of removal.
