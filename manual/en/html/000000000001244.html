Cylinder Head Installation
Install the cylinder head in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Clean the cylinder head, piston head, and block surface.
  1. Inspect the cylinder head warpage.
  1. Measure the piston head projection value for each cylinder using the dial indicator.
    -1.
    Set the dial indicator (A) on the block.
    -2.
    Zero the dial indicator at the point B on the block, then measure the projection value at the point C on the piston head. Do the same procedure at the point D, after zero the indicator at the point E.
    -3.
    Average of the measured values.
    -4.
    Measure the piston head projection value at other three pistons, and note the highest piston head projection value.
  1. Select a cylinder head gasket according to the highest piston head projection value.

  1. Clean and install the oil control orifice (A) with new O-ring (B).
  1. Install the dowel pins (C) and new cylinder head gasket (D).
  1. Set the crankshaft to top dead center (TDC). Align the TDC mark (A) on the crankshaft sprocket with the pointer (B) on the block.
  1. Install the cylinder head on the block.
  1. Measure the diameter of each cylinder head bolt at point A and point B.
  1. If either diameter is less than 12.5 mm (0.492 in.), replace the cylinder head bolt.
  1. With replacing the engine block. Apply new engine oil to the threads and flanges of all cylinder head bolts, and torque the cylinder head bolts in sequence to 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft), then tighten cylinder head bolts in two steps (100 ° per step). Loosen the all cylinder head bolts.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the threads and flanges of all cylinder head bolt.
  1. Tighten the cylinder head bolts in sequence to 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft). Use a beam-type torque wrench. When using a preset-type torque wrench, be sure tighten slowly and do not overtighten. If a bolt makes any noise while you are torquing it, loosen the bolt and retighten it from the first step.
    Specified Torque: 49 N·m (5.0 kgf·m, 36 lbf·ft)

  1. After torquing, if using the used cylinder head bolt, tighten all cylinder head bolts in three steps (90 ° per step). If using a new cylinder head bolt, tighten at 110 ° on the first step, at 100 ° on the second and third steps.
    NOTE: Remove the cylinder head bolt if you tightened it beyond the specified angle, and go back to Step 9 of the procedure. Do not loosen it back to the specified angle.
  1. Tighten the two 6 mm bolts securing the cylinder head.
  1. Bleed the air from the hydraulic tappet.

  1. Install the intake manifold.
