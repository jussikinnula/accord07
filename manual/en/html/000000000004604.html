HFT Switch/HFT-Voice Control Switch Test/Replacement
NOTE: Information marked with asterisk (*) applies to the switch with adaptive cruise control and navigation system.
  1. Remove the driver's airbag.
  1. Disconnect the 5P connector (A).
  1. Remove the two screws and HFT switch or HFT-voice control switch * (B).
  1. Measure resistance between the No. 3 and No. 2 terminals in each switch position according to the table.

    HFT Switch (Terminal No. 3 and No. 2)

    Position
    Resistance
    OFF
    About 1 kΩ
    HFT Talk button pressed
    About 180 kΩ
    HFT Back button pressed
    About 47 kΩ


    Navigation Voice Control Switch (Terminal No. 1 and No. 2)

    Position
    Resistance
    OFF
    About 10 kΩ
    Talk button pressed
    About 2.2 kΩ
    Back button pressed
    About 680 kΩ

  1. If the resistance is not as specified, replace the switch.
