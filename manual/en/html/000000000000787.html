M/T Differential Backlash Inspection
  1. Place the differential assembly on V-blocks (A), and install both axles.
  1. Measure the backlash of both pinion gears (B) with a dial indicator (C). If the backlash is not within the standard, replace the differential carrier.
    Standard (New):
    0.05−0.15 mm (0.002−0.006 in.)
