NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Use the appropriate tool from the KTC trim tool set to avoid damage when prying components.
  • Take care not to scratch the front bumper.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. From the back of the front bumper (A), release the hooks (B), and pull out the front bumper side cover (C).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and push the hooks into place securely.
