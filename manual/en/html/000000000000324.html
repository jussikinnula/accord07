DTC Troubleshooting: 2
DTC 2:
 A Short in the Air Mix Control Motor Circuit
  1. Disconnect the air mix control motor 7P connector.
  1. Disconnect audio-HVAC module 22P connector.
  1. Check for continuity between body ground and the audio-HVAC module 22P connector terminals No. 9, 13, 14, 17, and 18 individually.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair any short to body ground in the wire(s) between the audio-HVAC module and the air mix control motor.■
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II), and check the same terminals for voltage.
Is there any voltage?
YES -
Repair any short to power in the wire(s) between the audio-HVAC module and the air mix control motor. This short may also damage the printed circuit board. Repair the short to power before replacing the printed circuit board.■
NO -
Test the air mix control motor. If the air mix control motor is OK, substitute a known-good audio-HVAC module, and recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away, replace the original printed circuit board.■
