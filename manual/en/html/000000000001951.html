CMP Pulse Plate Replacement
  1. Remove the camshaft position (CMP) sensor.
  1. Remove the cylinder head cover.
  1. Hold the intake camshaft with an open-end wrench, then loosen the CMP pulse plate mounting bolt.
  1. Remove the CMP pulse plate.
  1. Install the CMP pulse plate and bolt.
  1. Hold the intake camshaft with an open -end wrench, then tighten the bolt.
    Specified Torque:
    12 N·m (1.2 kgf·m, 8.7 lbf·ft)

  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
  1. Install the CMP sensor.
