Cylinder Head Cover Installation
  1. Thoroughly clean the head cover gaskets and grooves.
  1. Install the new head cover gaskets (A) in the grooves of the cylinder head cover (B).
  1. Check that the mating surfaces are clean and dry.
  1. Apply liquid gasket, P/N 08C70-K0234M or 08C70-K0334M, on the chain case mating areas.
    NOTE: Do not install the parts if 4 minutes or more have elapsed since applying liquid gasket. Instead, reapply liquid gasket after removing old residue.
  1. Place the cylinder head cover (A) on the cylinder head, then slide the cover slightly back and forth to seat the head cover gaskets.
  1. Inspect the cover washers (B). Replace any washer that is damaged or deteriorated.
  1. Tighten the nuts in two or three steps. In the final step tighten all nuts, in sequence, to 9.8 N·m (1.0 kgf·m, 7.2 lbf·ft).
  1. Install the power steering hose clamp (A) and breather hose (B), then remove two bolts (C) securing the vacuum line.
  1. Install the bolt (A) securing the fuel feed line, and install two bolts (B) securing the fuel return line.
  1. Install the turbocharger boost control solenoid (C).
  1. Install the four injectors.
  1. Check that all tubes, hoses, and connectors are installed correctly.
  1. Install the engine cover.
  1. After assembly, wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
