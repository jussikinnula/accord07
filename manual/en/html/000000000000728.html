DTC Troubleshooting: 83
DTC 83:
 ECM/PCM
  1. Check for other DTCs.
Is DTC 86 indicated?
YES -
Do the troubleshooting for the DTC 86.■
NO -
Go to Step 2 .
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Disconnect the HDS from the 16P DLC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II) again.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle.
Do the VSA and VSA activation indicator come on, and is DTC 83 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure; the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check the PGM-FI system.
Does the MIL indicator come on or is the ECM/PCM's DTC indicated?
YES -
Do the applicable troubleshooting for the ECM/PCM.■
NO -
Go to Step 7 . (A/T)
Check for loose terminals at the ECM connectors, and go to Step 8 . (M/T)
  1. Check the A/T gear position indicator.
Does the D indicator come on while neutral position (N) selected or is PCM's DTC indicated?
YES -
Do the applicable troubleshooting for the PCM.■
NO -
Check for loose terminals at the PCM connectors, and go to Step 8 .
  1. Clear the DTC using the HDS.
  1. Disconnect the HDS from the 16P DLC.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, then turn it ON (II) again.
  1. Test-drive the vehicle.
Is DTC 83 indicated and no ECM/PCM's DTC stored?
YES -
Replace the VSA modulator-control unit. ■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
